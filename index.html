Riding FAQs

Do you have a minimum age?

The minimum age limit at the stables is 4 years old.

Do you have a weight limit?

The maximum weight limit for riders is 13 stone / 180 lb / 80kg - please note our weight limits are strictly observed.

What should I wear?

Wear long trousers to protect legs from chafing against the saddle. Avoid any clothing that could get tangled in equipment including scarves, thin tank top straps, and long, loose sweaters or shirts.

What equipment is included?

We provide boots and helmets as well as rain jackets and/or trousers.

What if I need to cancelation a ride?

When we take a booking we reserve a horse and instructor for our clients therefore our rides are non-refundable.

Can I change date of a ride?

We require 1 week notice to reschedule a weekend booking and 2 days notice to reschedule a weekday booking.
